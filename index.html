LockerMate: An app to keep track of your locker number

Never forget your gym locker number again! LockerMate is your smart companion for every workout, making locker management simple, fast, and stress-free.

Key Features:

  • Instant Locker Tracking: Enter your locker number, and LockerMate will remember it, so you can focus on your workout, not your memory.
  • Live Activity & Lock Screen Widget: See your current locker and session timer right on your Lock Screen or Dynamic Island. No need to unlock your phone!
  • Session Timer: Track how long you’ve been at the gym with a real-time timer. End your session with a tap and see your workout duration.
  • Automatic History: LockerMate saves your locker usage history, so you can review past sessions, durations, and even your favourite locker.
  • Personalised Statistics: View total gym time, average session length, and your most-used locker at a glance. Enjoy a clean, modern interface that looks great in both light and dark mode.
  • Privacy First: All your locker data stays on your device—no accounts, no tracking, just convenience.

Why LockerMate?

No more scribbling locker numbers on scraps of paper or fumbling with your phone. LockerMate is the easiest way to keep your gym routine organised and stress-free.
